★ The Plan, Briefly
Tent camping at Loop B Site #1 — the shaded, tent-only loop closest to the Gulf. Surf lessons for Ashley and the girls, a 6am inshore charter with Capt. Tony, kayaking Big Lagoon, a sunset glow paddle, and a whole lot of beach. New moon for the first half — stargazing will be excellent.

★ Week Rhythm
Mornings = water (calm Gulf, no wind, fewer bugs). Midday = shade + pool/AC retreat. Evenings = beach walks, fires, stars.
Sun · Jun 21 · Arrival
Get to the Island
Drive in. Publix stop on Gulf Breeze Pkwy. Pitch camp. Sunset beach walk. Easy dinner.
Mon · Jun 22 · Settle
Find the Rhythm
Beach morning. Walk to Fort Pickens itself. First proper dinner. Lights out early.
Tue · Jun 23 · 🏄 Surf Day
Lesson with Innerlight
8am surf lesson for Ashley + girls. Grab boards for the week. Lunch on the boardwalk. Nap through the heat.
Wed · Jun 24 · 🏄 Surf Practice
Free Surf at Langdon
Morning surf with rented boards. Langdon Beach is steps from camp. Afternoon bike loop inside the seashore.
Thu · Jun 25 · 🎣 Charter Day
6am with Capt. Tony
Inshore charter, 4 hours. Trout, redfish, mackerel. Back by 11. Cook the catch over the fire that night.
Fri · Jun 26 · 🛶 Paddle Day
Big Lagoon + Glow
Morning kayak at Big Lagoon. Pool afternoon. 7:30pm Glow Paddle sunset tour — LED boards, ~90 min.
Sat · Jun 27 · Last Full Day
Pick the Favorite
Last surf, last beach, family dinner in town. Back to camp for the final fire.
Sun · Jun 28 · Pack Out
Head Home
Sunrise walk. Break camp by 10am checkout. Ruby Slipper breakfast in Pensacola. Long drive back.
