★ The Plan
Tent camping at Loop B Site #1 — the shaded, tent-only loop closest to the Gulf. Surf camp at Waterboyz for Ashley and the girls, 6am inshore charter with Capt. Tony, kayaking Big Lagoon, a sunset glow paddle, and a whole lot of beach.

🌙 Bonus This Week
New moon June 24–25 = darkest skies for stargazing on Wed/Thu nights. Sea turtle nesting is in full swing — look for tracks at sunrise. Bring red-filter headlamps so we don't disturb them.
★ Week Rhythm
Mornings = water (calm Gulf, no wind, fewer bugs). Midday = shade + AC retreat. Evenings = beach walks, fires, stars.

★ Reality Check
Gulf in June is usually small and glassy in the morning. Waterboyz pivots between surf, skim, and paddle skills based on conditions — they're honest that they can't guarantee rideable waves daily. This is ideal for beginners. The girls will pop up on the first or second day.
